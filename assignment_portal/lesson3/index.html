Site Purpose

Layton City Chamber

To help our community acheive our common goal of wealth, safety, and family, no matter how we look. This website provides citizens, businesses, and community organizers resources for communication, collaboration, and updates of important news and changes to our city. As such, we have a community updates page where we post the latest business and community news that impacts all. We also have a directory page with local business information, as well as a contact page where you can contact us, the chamber staff and board officals via our contact us form.
